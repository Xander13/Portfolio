Alex is a mid level designer at Hook based in Detroit, working on UX/UI, Ads, and more.

I specialized in interactive design, with a focus in experimenting and pushing ideas to the table.

Previously, I've interned at Instrument and also Punchcut.

My mindset

My latest mindset is "control your inertia". In fencing, opponents are always a wild card on the fencing strip. We need to learn and to figure out how we can out smart them. We want to change and tweak small things to make things better.

I love this mindset thinking because it help shapes design thinking. We are always thinking about destroying and recreating things when we just need to improve the products or ourselves. It almost forms an atomic habits of seeing and fixing.


My educational path

I graduated at RIT with BFA in 2021 as magna cum laude honors with field of study in New Meida Design. My main skills is UI/UX design and noodling a lot of spaggetti codes creating prototypes.

I also did inmmersion in Adveriting and PR to gain more marketing strategy into the working space. I also really enjoy working deeply in creating system designs at scale to create a empowering visual designs.

My educational path

I graduated at RIT with BFA in 2021 as magna cum laude honors with field of study in New Meida Design. My main design thining is UI/UX design and noodling a lot of spaggetti codes creating prototypes. I also did inmmersion in Adveriting and PR to gain more marketing stargigy into the working space. I also really enjoy working deeply in creating system designs at scale to create a empowering visual designs.


My mindset

My last mindset is "control your inertia". In fencing, opponents are always a wild card on the fencing strip. We need to learn and to figure out how we can out smart them. We want to change and tweak small things to make things better. I love this mindset thinking because it help shapes design thinking. We are always thinking about destroying and recreating things when we just need to improve the products or ourselves. It almost forms an atomic habits of seeing and fixing.


Other than Design life

On my other times, I am working hard in Fencing doing saber and working on my personal challanges of being a good athelete and team player. I have gain a lot of new skills in fencing that helps my design process at work.

I also love going to differnet events like Config meeting my familures or meet and greet new people. I also gone to other events like FITC and Adobe Max to keep my learning on the nose.

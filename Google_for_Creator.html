Create a web space to showcase how creators can create amazing things with Google products

Google for Creators aim to create a learning space to showcase their products as guide tips on how to be better creators. It all started with a YouTube channel and now it's a full landing page for everyone to learn and create new things with Google products.


Google for Creators has a lot of content that needs to be sorted in a cohesive way. I explored many ways how contents can work to help promote content creators create their assets. I would contribute and share with the team different ways to help align clients' briefs.





Things I've learned

I've learned a lot in the design process and understanding the project scope. The design challenge had a interesting caveat of creating learning space that uses Google products in a subtle way.


I want to thank my team for teaching me so much while I was in this project. I enjoyed sharing my design processes with the team and clients.

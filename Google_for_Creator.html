Create a web space to showcase how creators can create amazing things with Google products

Google Creators aims to create a learning space to showcase their products as a guide tips on how to be better creators. It all started with a youtube channel and now its a full landing page for everyone to learn and create new things with Google products.


Google for Creators has a lot of contents that needs to sort together in a cohesive way. I explored many ways how contents can work to help promote content creators create their assets. I would help and share the team different ways to help to align clients briefs.





Things I learned

I have learned a lot in the design process and understanding the project scope. The design challenge had a interesting caveat of creating learning space that uses google products in a subtle way.


I want to thank my team for teaching me so much while I was in this project. I enjoyed sharing my designs process with the team and clients.

Creating a space where people can share their wishes

Covid-19 was hard for people to be motivated due to being stuck at home all the time. Wishful aims to bring a space to reflect and share a wish in a digital wishing well. Users can see others' wishes that they were inspired to be.


As my role, I helped imagine, design, and coded the the UI element. I have also designed and coded an AR experience into Wishful to create a tangible product that people can play around with their own devices.






Finding the right color and type to creating a visual story

Our team wanted to make Wishful a Wonderlust space that feels like you're in a story book for dreams and aspirations to grow. We carefully choose hues of deep ocean blues and purple tones with serif fonts to help create an immersive deep ocean to the deep sky. The depths corresponds to openness that are limitless possibilities.



Being a hybrid Designer and Developer bridges gaps and communication barriers.

We only had two developers on the team, so Will and I helped them code out different areas of developments. I focused on coding out a lot of the front-end aspect of the website and how the motions will work. I have learned a lot of Javascript and using libraries to help deploy our ideas into reality. My favorite aspect of coding is getting comfortable using advance CSS, jQuery and web performances.


Things I learned

While working with the team, I enjoyed sharing and learning with the different skillsets at hand. We really had a great team and we all contributed well into the project. Goooo team Prism!

The designs

Fresh designs for reading data in more clear visual way.






02
Overview



Problem


How might we improve the click rates of getting the users to buy more DNA kits to help understand and take preventive measure for near future cancers.

Outcome


We designed a new pages refresh with their new design system language and found new opportunities to help them enhance the viewing experience.

Research


Our team has researched in figuring out how does Color fit into the health market matrix and explorations on design assets to improve their brand strategies.




Old Color website










03
Features





Custom illustrations helps build the Color brand stronger

As Color mission is providing health information to users, our goal is to make sure they feel at ease and welcome reading heavy and possibly triggering topics.


The old site had no visuals that makes users connect to the readings. It was just all heavy data and text based.





People with smiles helps users see themselves that they can be happy in learning the Color pages. Avoid the churn and fearing in reading cancer facts.










I have done explorations on ways to improve on Color's new design language system because it was lacking visual importance for the users.




Red showcase harm as article explains the harmful effects of things.



Icons with no color background doesn't give emphasis to the context for users to grasp in quick glimpse.

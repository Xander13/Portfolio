02
Overview



Problem


How might we improve the click rates of getting the users to buy more DNA kits to help understand and take preventive measures for near future cancers.

Outcome


We designed a refresh to new pages with their recently developed design system language.

Research


Our team has researched how does Color fits into the health market matrix and explored on design assets to improve their brand strategies.




Old Color website










03
Features





Custom illustrations helps build the Color brand stronger

Color missions to provide health information to users to make sure they feel at ease when reading about difficult topics.


The old site had no supporting visuals for users connect to the readings. It was all heavy data and text based.





People with smiles helps users see themselves that they can be happy in learning the Color pages. Avoid the churn and fearing in reading cancer facts.










I have explored ways to improve on Color's new design language system as it was lacking visual importance for the users.




Red emphasizes harm as article explains the detrimental effects of diseases.



Icons with no color background doesn't give emphasis to the context for users to grasp in quick glimpse.

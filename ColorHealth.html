Color is a heathcare provider help bring acccesible healthcare knowage to people about cancer and genetics.

This was a client based project

3 Months

Update old Color Cancer webpages to thier new design system

Team

PM : Debashis Mazumder

Design Director : Paul Lafleur

The ask

Improve our existing Colors' cancer pages and improve the page flow.

01

Understanding the weakpoints

The problem

After aduting the 10 different cancer sites, I noticed it wasn't aiding to sell thier products or help reader with ease of reading.

How might we improve the experiance of the Cancer sites while doing the design refresh?

04

Reflections

What have I learned

Insights to Action

I have learned to take research insights and turn them into actionable steps in a marketable approach. Our project had no creative restrictions, so we needed to define our scope and create a design based on that.

Shaping the user flows

As I was learning to conduct my research, I learned the mental model of fitness was far different then I thought it would be. I learned so much taking what users inisghts about life at the gym or doing fitness has helped me reformed my thinking.

The Pivot

While working on this project, I had to rethink my process for accumulating users' fitness point tracking. With the help of gamification, I was able to tokenize point tracking into charges ⚡, making it something users can easily understand—energy power usage ⚡.

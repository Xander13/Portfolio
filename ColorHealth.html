Color Health Webpages

Improving Color Websites with revamped designs system and custom illustrations.


Time: 3 Months



Role: Client, Visual Design



Devices: Web Desktop, Web Mobile



Team:

Debashis Mazumder – PM

Paul LaFleur – Design Director

Alex Kauffman – Designer



Color is the only solution that combines early detection and comprehensive cancer management before, during, and after a diagnosis. As Color website promotes helpful and impacful information to help users buys DNA kits and care packages.


Client has asked us to work on a cancer site refresh because they were very outdated in designs. The site had no color verity which can lead very dry reading experiance.





The outcome of this project helped me better be better story teller creating custom illustrations and improve my implimenation of Color's design system into new fresh pages to give impacful readings.

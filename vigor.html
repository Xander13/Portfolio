Brief

This project was a very open interpreted project. I had to figure out what my motives were and what I wanted to accomplished in this project. Our project ask was Create a Fitness App. There wasn't any more to the ask for but other than what devices we had to design them for. It either could been made for Apple Tv or Apple watch along side the mobile device requirements.

As starting this project, I explored how I want to solve for a problem. I kept thinking about cross culture how some countries are much slimmer than American. That thought process has really struck with me and I wanted to find a solution for that.

Observing many videos and online readings, I find it amazing that Chinese culture really believe in exercising in late phase of age. Wheres America tend to stop when they get to busy in their life.

Observation
process

Research

After formulating my key areas I want to research and explore for my project, I conducted 20+ people to interview to gain more understanding why busy Americans with their day to day is like.

Some key points different interviewers has said:

"I don't feel comfortable being a the gym because gymtimidation is real as it can be. I feel pressured when doing a workout for myself"

"I don't like going to the gym because why should I waste an hour or two in a space I don't really value for. I rather take a bike ride in the woods."

"If I can't schedule my workout time in my calendar, I will forget to do it. So I must put down my workout time into my calendar."

These insights has help me understand why Nationally, 41.9 percent of adults have obesity. ( 1 ) are struggling to maintain their fitness. American working culture is busy hustle lifestyle to keep up with the living standers.

Also, American unhealthy dietary is also another major factor that Americans are increasing weights drastically. With busy lifestyle, more people are grabbing unhealthy fast food as they don't make time to cook. Same goes for not going to the gym.

So let improve that.

The
Flows

When I was formulating my goals, based on research and interviewers, I had a few key objectives I wanted to stress on:

Working out

This flows helps users get the exercise they need. From the research and goals in mind, this flow focus on crating a auto-populated workouts will help save users time. We want them to do workout not discouraged them.

Understanding the users needs - Onboarding

When singing up on the Vigor app, it's important to understand users health and workout needs so it can populate an appropriate workout to avoid burn outs.







The
Design

I started with creating moodboard that expresses about how we feel at working the gym.

The moodboard I was imagining was ice cold water we get for working out. The reason I went with a cooler color style because I didn't want to add heat into their workouts. I only added warmer color tone to add friction to warn users they are exiting a workout.

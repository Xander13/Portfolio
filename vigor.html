02
Overview

I explored how people from different cultures/countries are more fit than Americans.







Problem


How might we improve the way Americans workout

Outcome


I designed a fitness app that helps busy American workout wherever they wish and minimize their burnouts.

Research


I have conducted interviews, researched color theories, and explored ways to create this fitness app to be a more friendly experience for busy Americans.








03
Research

After formulating my key areas to research, I conducted 10+ interviews to understand their busy day lives.

04
Goals

Automation

Avoids users needing to surf the contents to find the workout they need. Minimizing the frustration to get them to start working out.

Regulated

Create a regulated system that helps users keep on track with their workout needs. With AI and Data of users daily inputs can help reduce burnouts.

Accessibility

Making sure product is accessible by creating a safe space for users to perform their exercise.





04
Design Process



When creating my moodboard, I wanted to utilized blue as a cooling effect to the design.



Creating the systems helps me declare my UX patterns which are buttons vs toggles to visual hierarchy.



Seeing the system come to a whole app experience.





05
User Flows



Based on the research and goals, I want to make sure the App minimize the gymtimidation for users.




This flow focus on getting into a workout routine users want. I made the workout as automated as possible.



This flow focus on determining the user athletic ability so the app can setup their workout needs.















05
Features

Experimentation on friction

As users are working out, they can get clumsy with their phones while scrolling through text music etc. I added a friction in this flow to help to help out uses confirm they are exiting out or pressed the wrong button.

Also I made buttons really larger to make it easy to toggle through each sections of the workout or Pause their activity.















Progress is everything

Using Vigor, based on users' input in the sign up has helped shape the app to reduce the users churn rate.

Minimizing the churn rate will assist busy users to Maintaining their fitness journey.







Design are always built for mobile use, Tv screen design is important to make Vigor workout accessible and inclusive for everyone.

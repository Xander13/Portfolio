Vigor is an app that can be used on your phone or apple tv screens. Vigor aims to create more accessible work out space for people to get their daily exercise.

01 Research

Nationally, 41.9 percent of adults have obesity. ( 1 )

Americans have high amount of obesity. The world of today is very convenient for people to grab unhealthy food to being to busy not able to cook and working out. These factors are reason why people have harder time going to the gym.




Target Groups

My target groups are adults. I have researched that adults need to retain good shapes because their metabolic rate slows down.


I've conducted interviews understanding what adults of age 20+ like to do when they are not doing any gym focus work.


The insights I have collected, help me shaped my reasoning for Vigor app imagine.

02 Design

My design styles based on feelings how we work out. Cold as refreshing ice drink we all enjoy after a hot flaming work out. I on purpose use red as contrast to the cool blue to give users attention to a specific element.










Design Trials

As the UI has been built together, I had to revised a few time to help create a better and easier working out sessions.


My first design had an accordion style to hide and unhide contents which would lead to miss tapping and stress for users not knowing the next step in timely manner. The new design is an automated scrolling that will move based on the timer. Users can skip based on the skip button.


I also added a red backdrop as warning when they pull down the workout page to bring more cautious over than a x button on the design.

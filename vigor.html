00 Solutions

Vigor is an app that can be used on your phone or Apple tv screens. Vigor aims to create more accessible work out space for people to get their daily exercise.


Vigor

/’vig-or/ – Adjective

Strong, healthy, and full of energy.

Vigor logo represents as a heart shape because there has been an increase in heart disease due to obesity.

Automated
workout station

Follow the workout steps with its timely routine step process.


Users can pause or skip a specific workout. If they skip they won't obtain that point but can redeem the rest.

To avoid burn out, Vigor regulates your workout amount

Most busy adults will initially overdo their workout sessions and will put it on hold for a month or more. From research I collected this is one key factor people don’t work out in a

This feature is key object from my research. I wanted to make sure the apps help them have consistent workout patterns.

Point system is the core of Vigor

Points are important because they help track progress and assist in keeping aware of not overdoing workouts.


Vigor helps connect with existing workout devices making it a centralized fitness tool.

Imagined for Apple TV

Also from my research, to many people are not motivated to travel to the gym for various reasons. Vigor can be set up on Apple TV conveniently located at home.




01 Research

Nationally, 41.9 percent of adults have obesity. ( 1 )

In America, there has been an increase in Obesity. With busy lifestyle, more people are grabbing unhealthy fast food as they don't make time to hook. Same goes for not going to the gym.




Target Groups

My target groups are adults. I have researched adults metabolic rates as it slow down considerably with age causing many health issues. Therefore, adults need to exercise consistently.


I've conducted interviews with adults ages 20+ to understand why they are not going to the gym.


The insights they gave me, helped me shaped my reasoning for Vigor app.




Motivation

My motivation is from the Chinese culture is very active community.


My design thoughts are driven on how we can find better more convenient ways to workout. Pleases we don't imagine. Removing the the needs of gym places so we can workout anywhere.

02 User flows

I designed Vigor layout to be easy for users to comprehend the needs they are interested in. Many existing market apps involve a lot of surfing to find the content users want/need.


Vigor helps minimize surfing with the populated workout programs. Also, along with populated workout it corresponds with tracking how much exercising users are getting to avoid burn out.




03 Design

My design styles are based on feelings. Cold as a refreshing ice drink we all enjoy after a hot flaming workout. I purposely use red as a contrast to the cool blue to give attention to a specific element.














04 Design trials

I had to revised a few time to help create a better user experience to help create an easier working out sessions.


In my first design, I had an accordion style to hide and unhide contents which would lead to overlooked assets, miss tapping, and more stress to the workout.

The new design is automated scrolling that moves based on the timer. Users can skip a step if they desire by activating the skip button.


I also added a red backdrop as warning when they pull down the workout page to bring more cautious than an X button on the design.

06 Things I learned

I have learned a lot in this project. I enjoy taking insights understanding different culture how they perform their fitness. I wanted to make a more accessible workout app to help American overcome obesity.


Around the time I created this app concept, Apple was just about to release Apple Fitness+ to the public which was quite cool we are helping people to be better with their health.


If I have to go back into this project, I would work more on creating this app idea, I would focus on the typography and custom typeface. Giving more focus on the brand to help improve the experience.

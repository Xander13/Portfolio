The Approch

When I was starting this project, I remembred about how other countries are so much more healither than Americans. I explored how people from different cultures/countries are more fit than Americans.









Problem


How might we improve the way Americans workout

Outcome


I designed a fitness app that helps busy American workout wherever they wish and minimize their burnouts.

Research


I have conducted interviews, researched color theories, and explored ways to create this fitness app to be a more friendly experience for busy Americans.

Design Process

Automation


Avoids users needing to surf the contents to find the workout they need. Minimizing the frustration to get them to start working out.

Regulated


Create a regulated system that helps users keep on track with their workout needs. With AI and Data of users daily inputs can help reduce burnouts.

Accessibility


Making sure product is accessible by creating a safe space for users to perform their exercise.





Designing UI elements to be chunky and approachable makes interactions more user-friendly.

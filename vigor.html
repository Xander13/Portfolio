Vigor Fitness


Vigor is fitness app that helps maintain workouts for people to avoid burn outs. Vigor app aims to help with busy Americans trying to shred off weight in more maintainable and safe way.


Role

UX/UI Design, Research

Challenges

Create a fitness app to your own interpretations on how you want to accomplished it.

Duration

One semester

automated timer

A regulated workout app that helps avoid workout burnouts

Weekly activity trend to keep users in a regulated workout sessions

Vigor splash screen to keep app private for users switches to other app modes

Creating fitness right at home on the tv

This project was an open interpreted project. I had to figure out what my motives were and what I wanted to accomplished.

The ask was to create a fitness app. We had to design them for iPhone as mobile first design and along with either Apple Tv or Apple Watch.




Starting this project, I explored how people from different cultures/countries are more fit than Americans.




Observing many videos and readings, I was amazed that the Chinese fitness culture really believe in exercising in late phase of age. Wheres America tend to stop exercising when their lives get busy or it's a trending activity.

02   â€“   Goals



From the study conducted, I've narrowed down my scope into three key areas of interaction: Automotive, Smart, and Accessible.



Automation

Avoid users needing to surf the contents to find the workout they need. Minimizing the frustration to get them to start working out.

Smart

Create a smart system that helps users keep on track with their workout needs. With AI and Data of users daily inputs can help reduce burn outs.

Accessible

Making sure product is accessible by creating safe space for users to perform their exercise.











03   â€“   Design Thought Process



The design was focused on the the experience as working out. I put a lot of focus on blue ton because it's the enjoyment of ice cold water and cooling effect. Blue also play in effect of color psychology of calmness, relaxation, and security.

Red in other hand is the opposite. I used the color of red to add friction and/or attention to the users to take actions.















04   â€“   The Flows



The working out flow focus on creating auto populated workouts which helps save time for the users. We want them to workout not discourage them.

Based on the research and goals, I want to make sure the App service the users to minimized the gymtimidation.





Experimentation on friction

As users are working out, they can get clumsy with their phones in pressing different sates, I added a friction in this flow to help users confirm they are exiting out or they could have miss-pressed the wrong button.

Also I made buttons really big to make it easy to toggle each sections of the workout or Pause their activity.





Progress is everything

With the sign up process, progress will shape based on users needs using AI to avoid burnouts.

As reflected, this is where we put a lot focus on making sure we can keep the app used in a consistent use other than just forgetting about it after burnouts.

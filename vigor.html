Vigor

Vigor is a fitness app that helps maintain workouts for people to avoid burn outs.


Role

UX/UI Design, Research

Tools

Figma

Challenges

Create a fitness app to your own interpretations on how you want to accomplished it.

Duration

1 semester project which is 15 weeks.

Constraints

Must be design based on Mobile and Apple TV

No creative restrictions, we have to find our movtives

02
Overview

I explored how people from different cultures/countries are more fit than Americans.







Problem


How might we improve the way Americans workout with...

Outcome


I designed a fitness app that helps busy American workout where ever they wish and minimize their burn outs.

Research


I have conducted interviews, researched color theories, and explore ways to create this fitness app to be a more friendly experience for busy Americans.








03
Research

After formulating my key areas I wanted to research and explore for my project, I conducted 10+ interviews with people to understand their busy day lives.

Interviewee 2

04
Goals

Automation

Avoid users needing to surf the contents to find the workout they need. Minimizing the frustration to get them to start working out.

Regulated

Create a regulated system that helps users keep on track with their workout needs. With AI and Data of users daily inputs can help reduce burn outs.

Accessibility

Making sure product is accessible by creating safe space for users to perform their exercise.





04

05
User Flows



Based on the research and goals, I want to make sure the App service the users to minimize the gymtimidation.

This flow focus on setting up the user athletic ability so the app can regulate their workout needs.















05
Features

Experimentation on friction

As users are working out, they can get clumsy with their phones in pressing different states, I added a friction in this flow to help users confirm they are exiting out or they could have miss-pressed the wrong button.

Also I made buttons really big to make it easy to toggle each sections of the workout or Pause their activity.















Progress is everything

Using Vigor, based on users input in the sign up has help shaped the app to reduce the users churn rate.

Minimizing the churn rate will help busy American keep maintaining their fitness journey. This is one of the goals I am tying to accomplish based on interview inputs.







Design is always built for mobile experience, Tv screen design is important to making Vigor workout accessible and inclusive for everyone.







06
The end



I have enjoyed project in working with users insights and collecting my motives how I can create the app ground up. I had to define a problem all the way to the final solution.

If I could go back into this project, I would have explored more how I would work on the point system to make sure users are comprehending the tracking units.

Apple Fitness+ just came out a few months after I have explored this topic. I find that really cool that people are creating solutions to help Americans have a healthy lifestyles at a large scale settings.

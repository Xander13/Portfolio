Brief

This project was an open interpreted project. I had to figure out what my motives were and what I wanted to accomplished. The project ask was to create a fitness app. We had to design them for iPhone as mobile first design and along with either Apple Tv or Apple Watch.

Starting this project, I explored how people from different cultures/countries are more fit than Americans.

Observing many videos and online readings, I was amazed that the Chinese fitness culture really believe in exercising in late phase of age. Wheres America tend to stop exercising when their lives get busy.

Observation
process

Research

After formulating my key areas I want to research and explore for my project, I conducted 20+ people to understand their busy day to day lives.

Some key points from different interviewers:

"I don't feel comfortable being a the gym because gymtimidation is real as it can be. I feel pressured when doing a workout for myself"

"I don't like going to the gym because why should I waste an hour or two in a space I don't really value for. I rather take a bike ride in the woods."

"If I can't schedule my workout time in my calendar, I will forget to do it. So I must put down my workout time into my calendar."

These insights helped me understand why Nationally, 41.9 percent of adults have obesity. ( 1 ) are struggling to maintain a healthy lifestyle. American working culture is busy hustle lifestyle to keep up with the living standers.

Also, American have unhealthy diets. With a busy lifestyle, more people are grabbing unhealthy fast foods. They aren't making time to cook or go the gym.

So let improve that.

Flows

When I was formulating my goals, based on research and interviewers, I had a few key objectives I wanted to stress on:

Working out

This flows focus on creating auto populated workouts which helps save time for the users. We want them to workout not discourage them.





Onboarding

When singing up on the Vigor app, it's important to understand users health and workout needs so it can populate an appropriate workout to avoid burn outs.




Singing In

When singing into the Vigor app, I wanted users to read facts so they are reminded why they are doing the workouts. Having self notes helps users keep aligns to their goals whey are not signed into the platform.







The
Design

I started with creating a moodboard that expresses how we feel when going to a gym.

The moodboard imaged was ice cold water getting ready to start the work out. I added warmer color to add friction when they are exiting a workout.

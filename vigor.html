Vigor



Intro



Vigor is a fitness app that helps busy Americans stay fit by offering automated, interest-based workout recommendations to prevent burnout.

The project was completed in one semester, starting with research and goal-setting, and turning insights into visual solutions.



The experiances requires two devices: an Apple TV or Watch, and a mobile device.

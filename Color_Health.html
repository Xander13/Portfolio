Brief

Color makes accessing healthcare simple for anyone, anywhere. The service helps people detect potential cancers and other genetic indifferences that may develop in the future.

The client asked us to explore new visual treatments to improve the overall UX of the sites. We have done competitive analysis and research to create new visual solutions to help them extend their design language system.

Observation
process

In design audit, I explored areas where Color can improve their marketing objectives. I found a few pain points on their old sites that needed improvement on web traffic.

The main thing they didn't have was enough contents to make it feel like it's a product page. Their main objective was to help make health care more accessible by selling DNA kits to users to learn about their genetics. The old site didn't feel like a company brand.

Pain points

The whole website was bland having a white background

Flat and simple illustrations doesn't make the users feel connected

In different areas of the site, there were unintended visuals that serve no purpose

Old design language system needed to be revamped to the most current Color's system

The
Designs

Color has provided nice start of color palate, iconography, and typefaces; however after building the new site with thier stuff, it still needed refinement that was necessary. The necessary things were improved hero illustrations, improved iconography to convey better meanings.

New illustrative hero photos

New visual visual hero created for the Color site enlightening the brand by focusing on DNA traits. The previous Color page, had no brand value to purcahse a DNA kit to learn about your genetic health. Users were disconnected when reading about the types of caners.

Refined iconography

From observation and competitive analysis, I was able to demonstrate different iconography treatment to elevate their design system. The client was pleased with the two tone color iconography style to convey a different meanings. The color tone in the iconography gives more impact to the design UI. In this scenario, we applied the color red to showcase harmful things that can cause caners.

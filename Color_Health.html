Creating a safer reading space

Medical jargon can create uneven tensions for users to read as they are trying to understand health complications. Color created a new design system that brings more lightness to the readers to enjoy the website.


As my role, I helped update their old existing Color webpages that had out of date visuals which were hard to read and inconsistent contents. I also created many new elements to help support their brand and their contents to help users enjoy the reading. I have worked on spot illustrations, researched iconography explorations, and creating fresh updated information graphs. I also helped clean up their page layout to make the contents flow better.






Bridging their new design language and pushing new contents to bring new exciting visuals

Researching different treatments on how we can improve our visual styles with Color's new visual language to help expand their starting point and pushed the brand in new areas.


I also worked on new fresh contents to help fill the missing information that users may have questions about.




Things I learned

While working with my design Director and client, I learned to create exciting visuals within the contents of the existing page and following the Color's style guidelines to help create new contents.

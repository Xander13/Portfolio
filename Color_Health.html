Brief

Color is a health client that sells DNA and other testing kits and help people improve users health to detects future potential cancers and other genetics indifference. Our main objective was to improve Color’s 8+ cancer pages with their new design systems.

They also have asked to explore new visual treatments to improve the over all UX of the sites. We have done competitive analysis researches and created new visual solutions to help them expand their design systems.

Observation
process

In my design audit, I have explored different areas how Color can improve with their marketing objectives. I have found few pain points that made their old sites lacking brand values.

The main thing they didn't have was enough contents to make it feel a product page. It was very distance to the company itself. Didn't really had any main points that helps users to motivate them buying the products to learn more about themselves. The site felt more distanced to read then the should feel connected to.

The
Designs

My role was implement new Color’s design system into their color sites. They had nice fresh color systems, clean iconography and other revamped things but it did lack other things to better the experience. After carefuly revewing thier design style guide deck, I found new opportunities to help push their design language to more well verse experience. Our goal was to help sell the product and help bring the users more related to the readings and learn new facts.

That’s where I came in to help them push forward their new deign language to new and enlightening visuals.

New illustrative hero photos

New visual hero creates the Color site more of an enlightening brand that is focus on DNA traits. The older color page, had no brand value to showcase to buy DNA kit to learn about your genetic health. It felt distanced to the users when they reading about the types of cancers.

Refined iconography

From observation and competitive analysis I have gathered, I have shown them may different iconography treatment to help elevate their design system. The client enjoyed the two tone color iconography style to help convey different meanings. The color toned in the iconography gives it a more impact to the design UI. As in this use case, they applied red color tone to showcase harmful things that can cause cancers.

What did
I learn

This project was a really enjoying working with their new design system and offering new solutions to them. I enjoy doing the research of competitive analysis.

I also learned to create more meaningful illustration to help give a better reading experience for the users. As a UX/UI designers we tend to get dry in our process leaving out illustrations as shown from older client examples. Applying fresh illustrations that works with their brand gives the site a new meaning. If they want to sell a product they have to make sure users are enjoying what they are seeing.

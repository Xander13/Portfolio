About

As a Product designer, he enjoys creating small things that make a big impact. He collaborates with large companies at Hook to create content that benefit the community at scale.



In Alexs' spare time, he fences saber around the USA, prototyping things with 3D printers, and also advocates for the Deaf community to empower their abilities in the workplace.
